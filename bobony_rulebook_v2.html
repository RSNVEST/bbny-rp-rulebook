CHAPTER 01

Roleplay Standards

R1
No Value of Life (NVL) Class B

Your character values their own life. You cannot act suicidally or ignore a clear lethal threat. If someone has a weapon pointed at your head at close range, your character understands that. You can still bluff, lie, or stall — but you cannot pretend the threat doesn't exist.

✓ Allowed
Negotiate under threat of a weapon
Resist if you have a realistic chance of survival
Bluff, lie, or stall for time
✗ Forbidden
Rush armed players while unarmed
Ignore a gun pointed at your head at close range
Act immortal when clearly outnumbered (2v1+)
R2
No Bait-Fighting Class C

Every act of violence needs a genuine prior RP reason. You cannot manufacture a provocation just to start a fight. Valid conflict requires a documented prior event that any admin can trace — a robbery, an insult, a territory violation, or a declared war.

⚠
Real Example — Forbidden: "Take off your hat or I kill you" followed by "insult your OG or we shoot you" with zero prior RP history = bait-fighting. This includes provoking players, fake situations, or any behavior designed only to bait violence. Class C offense.
R3
Stay in Character (No RP Break) Class B

Once an RP scene begins, you are your character until the scene ends. Breaking character during an active scene disrupts everyone involved.

  • OOC communication during a scene: Use the server OOC channel only.
  • Reacting OOC on stream: Streaming your own death and reacting out-of-character = RP break.
  • Discussing the fight in progress: Being in a Discord call with allies during an active scene = metagaming (R4).
R4
No Metagaming Class B

Your character only knows what they could realistically know from in-game events. Information that came from outside the game — a stream, a Discord message, voice recognition — cannot be used in-character. Your character and you are not the same person.

✓ Allowed
Watching your own past stream VODs after the session
Using in-game radio or phone calls
Sharing faction info via in-character calls
✗ Forbidden
Using a stream to locate enemies or loot
Using Discord to relay live fight positions
Knowing a player's identity because you recognize their voice
R5
No Toxic or Humiliating RP Class varies

RP creates stories — not a platform to humiliate real people. The class depends on the type of violation. Some acts are absolutely forbidden regardless of RP framing.

Type of ViolationClass
Insulting a downed playerClass B
Sexual RP without explicit consentClass E
Targeting a player's real life (appearance, personal situation, identity)Class D
Pure humiliation scene with zero narrative purposeClass C
R6
No Combat Logging Class B

Leaving the server during or immediately after an active RP scene is forbidden. "Immediately after" means within 10 minutes of the scene ending. This rule exists because disconnecting to avoid consequences destroys the RP experience for everyone involved.

  • Genuine emergency: Notify an admin via Discord BEFORE disconnecting.
  • Repeated "emergencies" that always coincide with losses = treated as intentional combat logging.
  • After an accidental crash: Return to your position and continue if the scene is still active.
CHAPTER 02

Combat & Conflict

R7
Valid Kill Rule (VKR) Class C

You need a valid, traceable in-game reason to kill another player. There are only three accepted conditions. Everything else is a violation.

Three Valid Conditions
  • Direct threat to your life — They credibly threatened your life or a gang member's life during an active RP scene.
  • Active declared gang war — War with documented history, acknowledged by owner or Head Admin.
  • Legitimate kill contract — Contract issued through official RP channels on the server.
Forbidden Kills
  • Kill on Sight (KOS) with no prior history
  • Killing a surrendered player who poses no threat
  • Killing a civilian unconnected to the conflict
  • Executing a player during an active cooldown period
R8
No Powergaming or Win RP Class C

You cannot use technical or scripting exploits to gain an unfair advantage in combat, or role-play in ways that ignore realistic limitations. RP is a shared story — not a competition to be won by any means necessary.

  • Teleporting, script abuse, or animation cancelling for combat advantage
  • Dropping items to avoid an inventory search
  • Unrealistic driving — Rocket League style or intentional VDM during RP scenes
R9
No VDM (Vehicle Deathmatch) Class B

Vehicles are not weapons. Intentionally running over players is VDM and is forbidden. No Rocket League driving: unrealistic stunts or ram tactics used in RP scenes are also covered by this rule.

R10
Must Record During Combat Class A

Players involved in combat, weapons use, or scouting must have recording software active. This protects both you and the players you're in conflict with. No recording = no proof = no refund.

📹
No clip = no refund on items lost in contested scenarios. Recording must include at minimum 2 minutes before and after the incident. If you cannot provide footage, admin decisions are final.
R11
Cooldowns After Combat Class B

After a combat scene ends, you cannot immediately re-engage. Cooldowns exist to prevent endless revenge loops and allow RP scenes to actually conclude.

SituationDurationRestrictions

Being downed (outside gang territory / police station)Entire sceneNo respawn and return to the same fight
After arrest / jailScene resolvedNo retaliation for the arrest without new RP justification
R12
Group Size Limits Class C

Active fighter counts are capped per scenario type. These limits apply to fighters on the ground — not total players watching or nearby. Exceeding the limit is immediately visible to admins reviewing recordings.

Zones & Locations

R13

The server is divided into four zone types. Each zone has different rules for PVP, robbery, and third-party involvement. Know the zone before you act.

  • No vehicle theft in Safe Zones under any circumstance.
  • Zone boundaries are marked in-game and on the Discord map channel.
  • Temporary event zones are announced 30 minutes in advance.
  • Unregistered territories have no zone protection.

R18
Alliance Rules Class C

Permanent alliances between gangs are forbidden. The server is built around independent gang dynamics, not bloc politics.

  • No permanent alliances: Gangs cannot form standing pacts to always fight together.
  • Temporary cooperation (one event, heist, or war) requires owner notification BEFORE it begins.
⚠
Combined Group Limit: Two cooperating gangs cannot each field 6 fighters. R12 limits apply to the combined force at all times. A two-gang operation still caps at 6 total active fighters.
R19
Gang Fight Protocol Class C

No gang fight is allowed without a prior meeting between both sides. This rule exists to prevent impulsive conflict that produces low-quality RP and repeated admin tickets. The meeting is mandatory — not optional, not skippable.

"One problem = One meeting = One fight."
  • No gang fight is allowed without a prior meeting between both sides.
  • During the meeting, no sneak attacks, ambushes, or surprise actions are permitted — it must remain a discussion.
  • The same issue cannot be used repeatedly to start multiple fights without new valid RP reasons.
  • Failure to follow this rule will result in strict administrative punishment.
R20
Territory Rules Class B

Territories can be contested — but not without notice. Surprise raids on registered territories are not valid RP.

  • Main gang territories can be attacked, but the attacking gang must inform the OG or a member of the defending gang at least 30 minutes in advance.
  • The territory map is visible on the in-game map at all times.
CHAPTER 07

Community Conduct

R21
OOC Respect Class B

Disagreements happen in RP. Disagreements in real life are handled through the ticket system — not public drama, not streams, not other servers.

  • Personal insults, threats, or harassment in-game are forbidden.
  • Publicly contesting admin decisions (Discord, stream, other servers) is forbidden.
  • Inciting drama on stream about admins or their decisions is forbidden.
  • Use the ticket system to contest any decision — respectfully and formally.
R22
Zero Discrimination Class E — Permanent Ban
Zero Discrimination Policy
ONE INCIDENT = PERMANENT REMOVAL

Any statement targeting a player based on gender, religion, ethnicity, nationality, or sexual orientation — in character or out of character — results in an immediate permanent ban.

No context makes this acceptable. Not RP. Not humor. Not personal familiarity.

This is not a graduated offense. There is no warning. There is no cooldown. One incident = permanent removal from this server.

R23
Female Player Policy Class D

Female players participate in the same RP on the same terms as everyone else. Their gender — in character or out — does not create special targeting rights or special restrictions.

  • No player may be excluded from or forced into RP scenarios based on their character's gender.
  • Sexual RP directed at any player without prior documented consent = permanent ban (Class E).
  • Harassment of female players outside the game is treated with the same severity as in-game violations.
R24
No Cheating or Script Abuse Class E — Permanent Ban

Using external tools, modified clients, or discovered exploits to gain any advantage is a permanent ban. Reporting an exploit you discovered is not just acceptable — it is expected. Using it is not.

✓ Allowed
Discovering an exploit AND reporting it to an admin
Cooperating fully with anti-cheat scans
✗ Forbidden
Discovering an exploit AND using it
Modified Windows or clients that bypass anti-cheat
Sharing cheat methods with other players
R25
No Ban Evasion Class E — Permanent Ban

A ban means you are not on this server. Using alternate accounts, or having friends vouch you back in while banned, is an immediate permanent ban on all accounts.

⚠
Complicity Rule: Knowingly vouching a banned player = formal warning. Repeat = temporary ban for complicity. You are responsible for who you vouch for.

CHAPTER 09

Punishment Ladder

Violations are handled progressively. Each level escalates from the last. Jumping levels is possible for severe violations — admins have discretion based on intent, history, and scope.

L1
Formal Warning
First / minor offense (Class A) No ban
Recorded warning. Logged in admin ticket history. Player notified formally. No ban, but the record follows you.
⟳ Clears after 30 clean days
L2
Short Ban
Second offense in 30 days OR Class B 48h ban
Warning stays on record. Player must acknowledge rules on return. Failing to acknowledge delays re-entry.
⟳ Clears after 30 clean days
L3
Extended Ban + In-Game Penalty
Third offense OR intentional bad-faith violation (Class C) 7-day ban
RP rank frozen for 14 days on return. Placed on Watchlist for 30 days on return. Any violation during Watchlist = immediate L4.
⟳ Clears after 60 clean days
L4
Major Ban + Demotion
Fourth offense OR Class D violation 30-day ban
Removed from RP activity (gang, police, EMS…) permanently. Placed on Watchlist for 60 days on return. Cannot hold leadership positions for 30 days.
⟳ Clears after 90 days
L5
Permanent Ban
Fifth offense OR Class E (cheat, hate speech, ban evasion) Permanent
Cannot be vouched back in. Single appeal to owner only, reviewed once. Any ban evasion after L5 = permanent with zero appeal.
✗ No reset — permanent record
